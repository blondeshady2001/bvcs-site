Our Logo

The turn of the 21st century marked the birth of the logo for the Beaver Valley Choral Society. Created by Regis Martin, one of our members, the logo depicts the heart and soul of our choir. We are dedicated to our motto through the performance of quality choral music. We invite everyone to "Share Our Joy, Share Our Song." Pictured above is Regis Martin (left) receiving a framed copy of the logo from Artistic Director, Phil Inman
